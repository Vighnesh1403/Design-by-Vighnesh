Odyssey Resorts

Designing an Activity Scheduling System for an Adventure Resort

Transforming a manual scheduling process into an intuitive planning experience.

Role

UI/UX Designer
(solo designer)

Team:

12 cross-functional members

Timeline:

2 weeks

Understanding the problem

Manual Scheduling

Staff relied on spreadsheets and manual coordination. Scheduling activities was slow and error-prone.

No Capacity Management

No clear visibility into available slots. Overbooking could easily happen.

Disconnected Experience

Booking information and activity scheduling were separated, making itinerary planning difficult.

The design journey

Iteration 01

The Seat-Booking Grid

My first instinct was to treat this like movie-ticket booking — a dense grid for picking time slots and checking availability across every activity.

Verdict

Functional, but review feedback called it heavy, cluttered, and hard to scan.

Iteration 02

Learning from BookMyShow

I studied how booking products like BookMyShow reveal complexity step by step instead of all at once, then rebuilt the flow around that idea.

Verdict

Cleaner and easier to follow, but the hierarchy was still busy — stakeholders wanted activities to feel connected, not isolated.

Iteration 03

The Version That Almost Shipped

I split Activity Scheduling from Booking Details into dedicated tabs, so staff could move between booking info and scheduling without losing context.

Verdict

The team was ready to build this version. But something still felt unresolved.

Iteration 04

The Breakthrough

I reframed the question from “how do we schedule activities” to “how do we help staff plan a guest’s day,” and introduced a Trip Planner that visualizes the whole journey.

Verdict

The team loved it, and so did the stakeholder. This became the version we shipped.

End-to-End Flow

The prototype demonstrates the entire scheduling workflow, including slot selection, capacity management, itinerary planning, and activity assignment.

What Improved

Redesign led to higher conversion, smoother flow, and happier users – backed by 4 key metrics.
