Key Learnings

Strong Visuals Build Trust

In photography platforms, premium visuals and cleaner layouts directly improve perceived quality and user confidence.

Simplicity Improves Decisions

Breaking complex booking flows into smaller guided steps made package selection and scheduling easier.

Scalable Systems Matter

Designing for multiple studio partners required flexible user flows that could scale without adding complexity.
