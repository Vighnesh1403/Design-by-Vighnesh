My Journey

I'm Vighnesh, a UI/UX Designer from Mumbai, India .

Looking back, design wasn't something I planned—it slowly became part of who I am. Ever since I was a kid, I loved drawing, painting, and creating things with my hands 🎨, long before I knew design could become a career.

During my second year of engineering, I made one of the biggest decisions of my life. Instead of following the expected path, I chose to pursue design. It was uncertain, a little scary, but deep down it felt like the right decision—and it's one I've never looked back on 🚀.

Over the last couple of years, I've worked on hospitality products, enterprise dashboards, booking platforms, and modern web experiences. Every project has taught me that great design isn't just about beautiful visuals—it's about solving real problems, thinking deeply about users, and obsessing over the little details that make everything feel effortless ✨.

Recently, I've been diving into motion design and frontend development. I love experimenting with interactions that bring interfaces to life and make digital products feel more natural, engaging, and memorable 🎬.

When I'm away from Figma, you'll probably find me playing the keyboard 🎹, watching anime 🍥, trying out new motion ideas, playing games with friends 🎮, or making friends with every dog or cat I meet 🐶🐱.

I'm still learning every day, still experimenting, and still chasing the feeling of creating products that people genuinely enjoy using. I believe the best designs aren't the loudest—they're the ones that simply feel right ❤️.
